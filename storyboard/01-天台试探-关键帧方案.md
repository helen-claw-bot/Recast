# 天台试探 — 关键帧方案

## 通用规则
- 🔗 **链式衔接**：Shot N 尾帧 ≈ Shot N+1 首帧
- 🏠 **环境锚点**：每帧至少含一个标志物（矮墙/薄荷/梅酒杯/暗蓝天空）
- 👤 **人物帧必须有人**：不让AI凭空生成人物
- 🎬 **硬切用剪辑**：角度跳转靠后期剪辑，不靠AI运镜
- 📐 **单镜头运镜 < 30°**：大角度变化 = 拆成两段

## 造型提示词（所有人物帧通用）
- **Leo**：East Asian man, late 20s, long wavy hair in loose bun, dark blue linen shirt (unbuttoned) over off-white sleeveless top, grey-brown straight trousers with rolled cuffs, woven bracelet on wrist
- **楚然**：East Asian woman, late 20s, long black hair loose, olive green short-sleeve utility shirt over white camisole, medium blue straight-leg jeans, brown leather crossbody bag, leather watch
- **环境**：rooftop at blue hour, low concrete wall, potted mint plants, Taipei city lights under low clouds, warm amber plum wine in glasses
