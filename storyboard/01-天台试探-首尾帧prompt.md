# 天台试探 — 首尾帧生成 Prompt（版本A·15镜）

> 环境基准：已确认天台空镜图（星星灯串、绿植环绕、纸箱木箱、阁楼暖光书架皮沙发）
> 所有 prompt 默认追加：cinematic, film grain, 16:9 aspect ratio, shallow depth of field, blue hour night

## 角色描述（复用）
- **Leo**：东亚男性，28-30岁，长卷发扎松散小揪揪，碎发垂耳前。墨蓝色棉麻衬衫敞开两三颗扣，内搭奶白色无袖圆领背心，灰棕色宽松直筒裤裤脚翻边，手腕编织绳手链，深棕旧皮靴
- **楚然**：东亚女性，26-28岁，黑色长直发自然散落微卷。军绿色短袖工装衬衫（翻领金属扣双口袋），内搭白色吊带，中蓝水洗直筒牛仔裤，棕色皮质斜挎小包，手腕皮表，白色帆布鞋

---

## Shot 1 — 推门（5s）

**首帧：**
从阁楼室内视角拍摄。前景是温馨阁楼内部——暖黄布艺落地灯、木质书架一角。中景是半开的木框玻璃门，楚然侧身站在门框中，右手推门，身体重心前倾正要跨出去。门外是天台——星星灯串、绿植、深蓝夜空。画面形成暖黄室内到冷蓝天台的色温对比。中景构图。

> Interior attic POV. Foreground: cozy attic interior, warm fabric floor lamp, wooden bookshelf edge. Mid: half-open glass door with wooden frame, young East Asian woman (Churan) in olive green utility shirt and blue jeans, standing sideways in the doorframe, right hand pushing the door, leaning forward about to step out. Beyond the door: rooftop with star-shaped string lights, green plants, deep blue night sky. Warm interior vs cool exterior color contrast. Medium shot.

**尾帧：**
同一视角。玻璃门已完全打开。楚然已走出门框，在天台上走了两步，背影朝向矮墙方向。星星灯串照亮她的侧面轮廓。门框仍在前景，形成画中画构图。

> Same interior POV. Glass door fully open. Churan has stepped onto the rooftop, back to camera, walking toward the parapet wall, two steps out. Star string lights illuminate her silhouette from the side. Door frame still in foreground creating frame-within-frame composition. Medium shot.

## Shot 3 — 倒酒（7s）

**首帧：**
特写。Leo的右手握着一瓶深色梅酒瓶，瓶身倾斜，琥珀色液体正在倒入一个透明玻璃杯。背景虚化——星星灯串化成金色光斑。能看到Leo前臂和编织绳手链。

> Close-up. Leo's right hand holding a dark plum wine bottle, tilted, pouring amber liquid into a clear glass. Background bokeh — star string lights as golden orbs. Visible forearm with woven bracelet. Warm amber tones against blue bokeh.

**尾帧：**
中近景。Leo侧面，刚放下酒瓶在矮墙上，面前两杯已倒好的梅酒。他的右手正把其中一杯向画面右侧推。能看到他的脸——长发小揪揪，墨蓝衬衫敞开露出白色背心。背景是星星灯串和城市灯光。

> Medium close-up. Leo in profile, just placed the wine bottle on the parapet wall, two filled glasses of plum wine before him. His right hand pushing one glass toward camera right. Visible face — long hair in loose bun, dark blue linen shirt open showing white sleeveless top. Background: star string lights and city lights.

## Shot 4 — 坐下·距离（3s）

**首帧：**
侧面中景。两人并排坐在矮墙边，面朝城市方向（背对镜头偏侧）。间距半个拳头——比朋友近，比恋人远。各端一杯梅酒。侧面轮廓被星星灯串的暖光勾勒。矮墙上的绿植在画面边缘。

> Side medium shot. Two people sitting side by side on the parapet wall edge, facing the city (angled away from camera). Half-fist gap between them. Each holding a glass of plum wine. Side profiles outlined by warm star string lights. Potted plants at frame edges.

**尾帧：**
同角度。楚然端起酒杯到唇边，Leo的姿势不变，目光微微转向她的方向。其余环境完全一致。

> Same angle. Churan raising her glass to her lips. Leo's posture unchanged but gaze slightly turned toward her. Environment identical.

---

## Shot 5 — "你刚才故意的"·正反打（10s）

**首帧（楚然）：**
近景，正面偏侧45°。楚然端着梅酒杯在胸前，目光看向画面左侧（Leo方向）。深蓝夜空做背景，星星灯串在她身后形成散焦光斑。表情是带着一点笑意的试探。

> Close-up, 45-degree angle. Churan holding plum wine glass at chest level, looking toward camera left (Leo's direction). Deep blue night sky background, star string lights as bokeh behind her. Expression: slight smile, playful probing.

**尾帧（楚然）：**
同角度。楚然嘴角更明显地上扬，眼神带挑衅——"你知道什么"说完的表情。酒杯位置略低。

> Same angle. Churan's smile more pronounced, eyes slightly challenging — the look after saying "you know what." Glass slightly lower.

---

## Shot 5b — 反打Leo（3s）

**首帧：**
近景反打，正面偏侧45°（镜像方向）。Leo侧头看向画面右侧（楚然方向），嘴角有一个不确定的弧度，像快要笑但忍住了。深蓝夜空背景+星星灯串散焦。

> Close-up reverse shot, 45-degree angle (mirrored). Leo turning head toward camera right (Churan's direction), corner of mouth in an uncertain curve — almost smiling but holding back. Deep blue sky + star light bokeh background.

**尾帧：**
同角度。Leo微微扬眉，嘴角的弧度稍大了一点。眼神变得更直接。

> Same angle. Leo's eyebrows slightly raised, mouth curve slightly wider. Gaze more direct.

---

## Shot 6 — "我确实是故意的"（10s）

**首帧：**
中近景。Leo端起梅酒杯遮住下半张脸，只露出眼睛和眉毛。杯中琥珀色梅酒透光。他的眼神从杯沿上方看向画面右侧。星星灯串在背景。

> Medium close-up. Leo holding plum wine glass up, covering lower half of face, only eyes and eyebrows visible above the glass rim. Amber wine catching light. His eyes looking toward camera right over the glass edge. Star lights in background.

**尾帧：**
同角度。Leo已放下酒杯（杯子放在矮墙上，画面下方边缘可见），露出全脸，表情坦然而温和。直视画面右侧。

> Same angle. Leo has lowered the glass (visible at bottom frame edge on the wall), full face revealed, expression calm and gentle. Looking directly toward camera right.

---

## Shot 7 — "我没说不舒服"（5s）

**首帧：**
极近特写。只拍楚然的嘴唇到下巴。嘴唇微闭，涂着很淡的唇色。下巴线条清晰。背景完全虚化成深蓝色。皮肤上有星星灯串反射的微弱金色光点。

> Extreme close-up. Only Churan's lips to chin. Lips slightly closed, very light lip color. Clean jawline. Background completely blurred deep blue. Faint golden light spots from string lights reflected on skin.

**尾帧：**
同角度。嘴唇微微张开后轻合，嘴角有极细微的上扬——刚说完"我没说不舒服"的瞬间。

> Same angle. Lips slightly parted then softly closed, corners of mouth with the faintest upturn — the instant after saying something unexpected to herself.

---

## Shot 8 — Leo的眼睛（3s）

**首帧：**
大特写。Leo的双眼。暗蓝色天光映在瞳孔里，能看到星星灯串的倒影在眼球表面。眼神是试探的、不确定的。长睫毛，眉骨深邃。

> Extreme close-up. Leo's eyes. Dark blue sky light reflected in pupils, star string lights visible as tiny reflections on the eye surface. Gaze is searching, uncertain. Long eyelashes, deep brow bone.

**尾帧：**
同角度。瞳孔里的光变亮了——"被点燃"的感觉。眼神从试探变成确认。眼角微微收紧。

> Same angle. Light in pupils brighter — an "ignited" quality. Gaze shifts from searching to certain. Slight tightening at eye corners.

---

## Shot 9 — 别头发（12s）

**首帧：**
中近景双人。Leo转身面对楚然，右手抬起，准备触碰她脸侧被风吹乱的头发。两人距离约20cm。楚然微微仰头看他。背景是矮墙、星星灯串、深蓝夜空。

> Medium close-up, two-shot. Leo turned to face Churan, right hand raised, about to touch her wind-blown hair on the side of her face. Distance about 20cm apart. Churan looking up at him slightly. Background: parapet wall, star string lights, deep blue sky.

**尾帧：**
特写。Leo的指尖停在楚然的耳垂位置。两人的脸很近，大约10cm。楚然的眼睛微微向下看他的手指。能看到Leo指尖和她耳垂之间几乎为零的距离。

> Close-up. Leo's fingertips resting at Churan's earlobe. Their faces very close, about 10cm apart. Churan's eyes looking slightly downward at his fingers. The near-zero distance between his fingertip and her earlobe is visible.

---

## Shot 10 — 楚然的呼吸（5s）

**首帧：**
近景。楚然的脸，正面微偏。呼吸明显加快（嘴唇微张）。眼神直视画面左侧——看着Leo的眼睛。星星灯串在背景形成温暖散焦。

> Close-up. Churan's face, slightly off-center. Breathing visibly faster (lips slightly parted). Eyes looking directly toward camera left — at Leo's eyes. Star string lights as warm bokeh in background.

**尾帧：**
同角度。楚然的眼神从Leo的眼睛下移到他的嘴唇位置（视线方向微微向下）。这是她做决定前的最后一刻。

> Same angle. Churan's gaze shifts downward from Leo's eye level to his lip level (line of sight angled slightly down). The last moment before her decision.

---

## Shot 11 — 最后的试探（15s）

**首帧：**
双人中近景，正面偏侧。两人面对面，距离很近（约15cm），背景是矮墙上的两杯梅酒和星星灯串。深蓝夜空。两人都在看着对方的眼睛。

> Medium close-up, two-shot, slightly angled. Two people face to face, very close (about 15cm), background: two plum wine glasses on parapet wall and star string lights. Deep blue sky. Both looking into each other's eyes.

**尾帧：**
同角度微推至Leo近景。楚然的脸在画面边缘虚化。Leo直视前方（楚然的方向），表情诚恳、脆弱——"我自己也分不清"说完的样子。

> Same angle, slightly pushed in to Leo close-up. Churan's face blurred at frame edge. Leo looking forward (toward Churan), expression sincere, vulnerable — the look after admitting something honest.

---

## Shot 12 — 薄荷与风（3s）

**首帧：**
特写空镜。天台上的一盆薄荷，叶子静止，水珠在叶面反光。星星灯串的暖光从上方洒下。背景虚化深蓝。

> Close-up, empty shot. A pot of mint on the rooftop, leaves still, water droplets catching light on leaf surface. Star string light warm glow from above. Background blurred deep blue.

**尾帧：**
同角度。薄荷叶子被一阵夜风吹动，向右轻轻摇摆，有两三片叶子翻转。水珠被抖落。

> Same angle. Mint leaves swaying gently to the right from a night breeze, two or three leaves flipping. Water droplets shaken off.

---

## Shot 13 — 吻（14s）

**首帧：**
侧面中近景。楚然身体微微前倾，朝向Leo。Leo微愣，身体还是原来的姿势。两人嘴唇距离约5cm。侧面轮廓被星星灯串勾勒。深蓝夜空背景。

> Side medium close-up. Churan leaning slightly forward toward Leo. Leo slightly frozen, body still in original posture. Their lips about 5cm apart. Side profiles outlined by star string lights. Deep blue sky background.

**尾帧：**
侧面近景。Leo的嘴唇贴在楚然耳根位置，他的右手在她后颈，左手扣住她的腰。楚然的眼睛半闭，头微微后仰。两人身体完全贴近。星星灯串虚化成光晕。

> Side close-up. Leo's lips pressed against Churan's ear, his right hand on the back of her neck, left hand on her waist. Churan's eyes half-closed, head tilted slightly back. Bodies pressed close. Star string lights blurred into halos.

---

## Shot 14 — "下楼"（7s）

**首帧：**
大特写。Leo的下半张脸贴着楚然的耳侧。能看到他的下颌线、嘴唇贴近她耳根的细节。两人的轮廓在深蓝背景中几乎融为一体。极浅景深。

> Extreme close-up. Leo's lower face pressed against the side of Churan's ear. Visible jawline, lips close to her ear. Their silhouettes almost merging against the deep blue background. Ultra-shallow depth of field.

**尾帧：**
同角度。微弱的呼吸起伏——Leo的下巴微动，像在说话（"下楼"）。楚然耳侧的碎发被他的气息吹动。其余不变。

> Same angle. Subtle breathing movement — Leo's chin shifts slightly as if speaking. Churan's baby hair near her ear stirred by his breath. Everything else unchanged.

---

## Shot 15 — 尾声（8s）

**首帧：**
中景。矮墙上两杯梅酒还在（酒少了一些）。薄荷叶在风中轻晃。星星灯串依然亮着。远处台北灯光模糊。玻璃门关上了，门内暖光变暗。天台上无人。

> Medium shot. Two plum wine glasses still on the parapet wall (slightly less wine). Mint leaves swaying gently in breeze. Star string lights still glowing. Taipei city lights blurred in distance. Glass door closed, interior light dimmed. Empty rooftop.

**尾帧：**
同角度。画面自然渐暗。星星灯串的光变得更突出。一切静止，只有风。准备黑屏。

> Same angle. Frame naturally darkening. Star string lights become more prominent as ambient light fades. Everything still, only wind. Preparing for black screen.
